Fresh Tomato, Sausage, and Pecorino Pasta
Ingredients

8 ounces uncooked penne, 8 ounces sweet Italian sausage, 2 teaspoons olive oil, 1 cup vertically sliced onion, 2 teaspoons minced garlic, 1 1/4 pounds tomatoes, chopped, 6 tablespoons grated fresh pecorino Romano cheese, divided 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/8 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 cup torn fresh basil leaves,


Preparation

1. Cook pasta according to package directions, omitting salt and fat; drain. 2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Remove casings from sausage. Add oil to pan; swirl to coat. Add sausage and onion to pan; cook 4 minutes, stirring to crumble sausage. Add garlic; cook 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes; cook 2 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in pasta, 2 tablespoons cheese, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup cheese and basil.


Where to Eat?

Dorry Pasta at İstiklal
